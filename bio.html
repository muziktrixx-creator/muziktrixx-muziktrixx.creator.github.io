Artist Bio

Muzik Trixx

Muzik Trixx is an Afro artist driven by real-life experiences, passion, and the hunger to rise. His sound blends Afro rhythms with deep, relatable lyrics inspired by love, hustle, gratitude, and personal growth.

Born from the vision of building something bigger than music, MUZIK €MPIRE represents a movement — creativity, consistency, and self-belief. With every release, Muzik Trixx continues to evolve, connecting with listeners who feel the story behind the sound.

From Sierra Leone to the world, Muzik Trixx is focused on impact, originality, and leaving a lasting mark in the Afro music space.
